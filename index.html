One bug to start them all...

It all started with just one small, but hard-to-find bug in the app that was owned by our
founder some years ago. Now though, with more than 40,000 testers from 150+ countries
across the globe, we support businesses with a crowdsourced testing platform with
custom-tailored solutions. Buglance speeds up the testing process in a unique way that
companies get their initial bug or issue reports in the shortest time as possible. Our
company is backed by 500Startups and TechStars - world-renowned organizations that support
innovative and disruptive startups.
